About HnT IIT Mandi

We are the Hiking and Trekking Club of IIT Mandi with more than 50 active trekkers. For an IIT in the lap of Himalayas, a full-fledged Hiking and Trekking club caters to the spirit of adventure that resides in the students of IIT Mandi. Himachal Pradesh is one of the most beautiful places on earth. Nature truly signifies its beauty in these set of mountains where our institute is situated.
